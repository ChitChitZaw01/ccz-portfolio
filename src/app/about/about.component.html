What we are doing now

As we are a group of educators, we focus on giving training and networking for higher education and career developing. We started as English proficiency class, but now, we are going to provide more classes. We plan to open class for the following courses:

1. Language class (English, Japanese)

2. ICT Class (Python, Web Design)

3. University Preparation (GED, SAT)

We expected to accept over 200 students in a year through these courses in our institute. We are also connecting partner universities for credit transfer so that students can pursue higher education.

Our Vision

We emphasize on empowering youths to have sufficient professional skills for their future career and help to shape their dreams to achieve. We make sure youths to get education rights that they deserve anywhere and anytime.

Our Mission

-To share knowledge about international education

-To help youths to explore their strength and weakness

-To open opportunities for youths having access to higher education and career choice

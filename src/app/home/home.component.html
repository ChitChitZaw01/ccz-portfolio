Welcome to Brilliant Educational Institute

BEI is a short form of Brilliant Educational Institute, which is a small organization founded in order to provide vocational training for Myanmar Youths who lost their education rights within their own country. BEI is composed of a group of young educators who wish to strive a change for the future generation. BEI was first established as Brilliant Education Center on social platform, and this education center has been giving language class since 2023 via online platform with affordable price.

It mainly focuses on giving English proficiency class starting from elementary level to academic training courses for nearly 2 years. Within 2 years, we have provided assistance to over 200 youths for their future purposes through the various courses.As the vulnerable population are growing, we are striving more to provide the necessary skills for them. The organization needs to grow for more room, so we decided to expand education center to vocational institute for the purpose of providing more courses.
